Videographer & photographer website

Type

College project

Role

UI/UX Designer, UX Researcher, Product Owner

Timeline

Mar 2018 - Current

The product is a portfolio website for a videographer/photographer.

After competitive research, the goal was to make this website stand out among so many others. Most of the websites were overloaded with information and graphics. Many didn't have clear pricing.

I talked to previous clients and it became more clear what kind of users needed this website, how they behaved, and what kind of info was necessary for them. That allowed to create personas and a user story map.

The project is still going on and now it's on the stage of ux writing.

Personas

Peter

  • Party promoter and organizer.
  • Communicates with lots of people and has a lot of things on his mind at once.
  • Usually does not have time to search for people on the Internet and go through their portfolios.
  • Finds people through recommendations and gives them a shot to see if that works for him.

Michael

  • Musician, plays in a band.
  • From time to time, needs a professional to promote his band.
  • When he chooses a professional, he goes deep in their portfolios watching their videos and reading about them.
  • Uses YouTube, SunCloud, all social networks: Facebook, Instagram, Twitter.

Barbara

  • Bride, getting married in 6 months and she wants create the wedding of her dreams.
  • Needs to feel comfortable on her wedding.
  • Follows wedding photographers and videographers on the Instagram to get to know what she wants.
