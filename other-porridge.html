Lines, space, and composition

Type

Typography course

Role

Designer

Timeline

May 2015

I took a course about typography — mostly about vertical lines, spaces, and composition. We had to make a page to sell something.

The highlights from the course:

  • Give a clear sense of the page structure with hierarchy: sizes of body text and headings
  • The less vertical lines, the easier to "scan" the page
  • Space is crucial for building relations between elements
  • The page needs to "breath" = have enough negative space
  • Line height is essential for readability
