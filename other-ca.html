Certificate authority

It's a service for individuals and companies to get a certificate authority (CA). CA means a digital signature to certify the ownership. Digital certificates are to report customers' tax data to State Tax Authority, to participate in electronic auctions, and to do other business-to-government things.

Making CA consists of several phases that include 6 groups of people to conform the legislation. Most of these groups are internal users. Every day, I caught feedback from all of them, so I've learned how to juggle and prioritise tasks.

Type

Long-term internal project

Role

Front End Developer first, then UI/UX Designer

Timeline

Oct 2010-Aug 2014

Private cabinet

An interface for clients to work with their data—to fill and send requests for certificates, to store certificates.

Registration centre

An interface for specialists communicating with clients. Clients come to them offline with documents, and specialists do paperwork, request for certificates, and track a process through the interface.

Certificate authority

The most important interface for operators checking data. They verify scans of documents and clients' personal data. The interface helps with instruments—loop and scaling.

Documentation centre

An interface for specialists receiving paper documents. They compare it to electronic copies.

Big Brother

An interface for very powerful people :) They have a full access to everything and solve the most difficult problems.

Technical support

An interface for specialists to help clients in difficult situations.
