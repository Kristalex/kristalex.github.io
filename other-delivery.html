CRM for Delivery Company

On this project, I worked directly with a product owner and a client.

Type

Short-term project

Role

UI/UX Designer

Timeline

It's a startup about delivering from different shops. I made an interface for a manager who tracks a delivery process. The main scenario is to watch how everything is going and to reveal problems. The manager also can edit couriers and shops.
