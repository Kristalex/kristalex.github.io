CRM for Delivery Company

On this project, I worked directly with a product owner and a client.

Type

Freelance project

Role

UI/UX Designer

Timeline

Jun-Jul 2013

The project was a startup. The client was a delivery company to be. They wanted to deliver products from pharmacies to customers. To do that, they were going to create an app for pharmacies to places their delivery orders and to hire couriers-contractors to deliver.

My job was to create a CRM to manage the whole delivery process.

Persona

Mark Katz

  • The only manager.
  • Works from 9am to 6pm in the office or at home, and on the weekends when needed.
  • Responsible for maintaining delivery, couriers, and stores data.
  • Not too tech-savvy. Great at speaking to people.

User story map

Solution

Due to the limited amount of time and budget, I didn't have a chance to conduct proper research and usability testing. I designed wireframes based on the main user stories and a business model.
