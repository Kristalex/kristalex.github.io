Word choice in web writing

Help readers find your content right away, read it easily, and use it without thinking

SEO

Write words that your readers use to search for your content. In that way, you will get your website higher than other websites with the same search terms.

Check out Google's Keyword Tool or Wordtracker

Specific terms

Dive into the world of your readers and speak their "language".

For example, if you are writing an article about medical research, it is better to write two articles — for patients and for doctors. Not for everybody.

Words in design

Use design patterns that are familiar for reader through the internet. For example, buttons should be verbs and links should say precisely where they lead to.

Be consistent so that readers always knew what you mean from their previous experience on you website.
