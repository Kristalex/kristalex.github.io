Word choice in web writing

Help users find your content right away, read it easily, and use it without thinking

SEO keywords and website rank

Writing words that your users use to search for your content will get your website rank higher than other websites with the same search terms.

Check out some SEO tools Google's Keyword Tool or Wordtracker

Specific terms

Make sure you speak the "language" your users speak and use their vocabulary.

For example, if you are writing a medical research article, it is better to write two articles — one directed towards patients and one towards doctors. Do not write for everybody.

Words in design

Use design patterns that are familiar to users through the internet. For example, buttons should be verbs and links should be clear about where they lead to.

Be consistent so that users always know what you mean based their previous experience on your website.
