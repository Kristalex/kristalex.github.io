Product

Market

In Russia, if companies need to buy goods or services, they place their needs on special websites. We call these needs purchases. Suppliers search for purchases to provide services.

Mission

We aggregate purchases and help suppliers to find them. After searching, we help to organize and analyse purchases.

Competitors

  • a free government website
  • a number of paid websites with the same goal

We bet on a good search and usability. In design and implementation of every feature, we followed UX best practices. Our strategy was not based on first to market, but best to market. And it paid of. The product is still growing and already has over 10000 users.

Solution

Searching for purchases

Searching by parameters is the first step for Dmitry and Irina. They work with a search form to make requests for their company.

Search form and results

Based on user testing and competitive research, the most important search fields are keywords and region.

Search results contain data essential for a decision to go deeper or not.

Advanced search
Only after the key scenarios did we pay attention to less frequent cases. Read about form's lifetime

After the first time, Dmitry and Irina save their searches and subscribe to them. Since then, they will receive new purchases by email every day which fastens the search process.

Saving and subscribing

To connect saved searches and subscription transparently, we made a continuous process—one control to make it step by step.

UX testings showed that it was much easier to subscribe right after saving a search.

Analytics showed that the amount of subscribed users increased considerably.

After the subscription, we saw that 60% of our users opened emails on their mobile phones. From there they went to view purchases and had a terrible experience because the website was not mobile-friendly. Therefore, starting with making the list of purchases mobile-friendly, we made a mobile version for the whole site.

Analyzing

Dmitry and Irina decide here whether a purchase is convenient for their company. If it is, they mark it as favourite. Dmitry's work is done at this moment.

Irina will come back to the purchase page to prepare documents. She will analyze a customer before participation to eliminate risks when the customer doesn't pay or always chooses the same suppliers. After participation, Irina will analyze the finished purchase to improve her strategy.
