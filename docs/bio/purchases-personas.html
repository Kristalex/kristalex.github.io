Personas and scenarios

Dmitry Ivanov, 32 years

Deputy Commercial Director, "Installation"
Bachelor's Degree in Law

"OOO Installation" is a small construction company. It has been in business for 5 years and has around 60 employees; roughly 9 of them are administrators and engineers. The company is widening its service.

Dmitry has irregular working hours, works at office or home, and often visits building sites. He has experience working with business software, internet banks, and websites with purchases. He works with MacBook Air running Windows OS.

Responsibilities and scenarios

  • He manages production processes.
  • He communicates with clients and partners.
  • He searches for purchases that fit company's services. He marks interesting purchases as favourites. Then, other specialists do all further work with those purchases.

Irina Petrova, 25 years

Senior purchases specialist, "EnergySK"
Bachelor's Degree in Energetic Technology

"EnergySK" is engaged in capital construction of energy facilities, repair and maintenance of electrical installations, and testing of electrical equipment.

Irina has been working in their purchase department for a year. She has had short-training courses for suppliers, but she studied the key aspects of purchasing on her own. The purchases department consists of 5 people.

Responsibilities and scenarios

  • She searches for purchases and marks favourites. She does it every morning 5 days a week.
  • She analyzes if it's worth participating in a purchase (customer is trustful, auction is fair). She might ask her director to help in this one.
  • She prepares documentation for purchases that fit and look valid.
  • She participates in purchases when it's auction time.
  • She analyzes finished purchases both where they participated and where not. It's the way to check customers and competitors.
