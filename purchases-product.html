Timeline

Market

In Russia, if companies need to buy goods or services, they place their needs on special websites. We call these needs purchases. Suppliers search for purchases to provide services.

Mission

We aggregate purchases and help suppliers to find them. After searching, we help to organize and analyse purchases.

Competitors

  • a free government website
  • a number of paid websites with the same goal

We bet on a good search and usability. In design and implementation of every feature, we followed UX best practices. Our strategy was not based on first to market, but best to market. It paid of. The product is still growing and already has over 10000 users.

User journey map

Solution

Search form and results

Based on user testing and competitive research, the most important search fields are keywords and region.

Search results contain data essential for a decision to go deeper or not.

Advanced search
Only after the key scenarios did we pay attention to less frequent cases. Read about form's lifetime

Saving and subscribing

To connect saved searches and subscription transparently, we made a continuous process—one control to make it step by step.

UX testings showed that it was much easier to subscribe right after saving a search.

Analytics showed that the amount of subscribed users increased considerably.

Purchase's data

We aggregated data from different sources and showed the most important using the same view to shorten the time needed for a decision.

Analyzing customer

A supplier can analyze a customer by their previous auctions: see competitors, who wins more often, if there's a fair competition.

It's a way to uncover an affiliation of participants, conditions of the auction, and a percentage of price reducing.

Favourite purchases

Besides just saving an interesting purchase, there's an option of marking a purchase differently. The feature was based on a user research where we saw how users added small notes to their purchases in Excel.

Changes in purchases

Customers change purchases. It's important for suppliers to be notified because it influences chances to win. We are the ones who have learned to identify changes and show it.

Landing page

In the beginning the service was free. We were extending our clients base and getting a feedback. This page appeared after we reached MVP.
